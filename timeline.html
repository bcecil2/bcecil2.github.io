Oregon State Investment Group Portoflio Management

Portfolio Research and Management

The Oregon State Investment Group (OSIG) currently manages nearly $3.5 million spread across 3 different portfolios. However unlike major finance firms, OSIG does not have access to the same advanced thechnology. Our Project seeks to change that. Upon our projects completetion the group will have access to software that, Expedites the portfolio research process analyzes and optimizes portfolio allocations, and Computes and displays essential portfolio performance metrics.
