One of the central differences between the Cathedral and the bazaar is the formers sense of mysticism that seems to be intertwined with closed source programs. Raymonds use of wizards is far from an arbitrary choice of words but rather an apt portrayal of the guild like attitude of closed source. Projects seemed to be built by many groups of few people each pundits but only in their specialized subsection of programming. In the stark contrast the bazaar ideology is just as it sounds bustling,full of trade and constantly shifting to meet demands. Where Cathedrals were not open until finished the bazaar offered constant betas and in doing so opened up the doors for constructive criticism and insight to mass amounts of people, while cathedrals stayed closed shut till opening day marinating, and offering little to no room for outsider input.
